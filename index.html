Valerie Gilchrist

(she/her)

Email: valerie.gilchrist@ulb.be // CV: here // Google Scholar: here

I completed my PhD at the Université Libre de Bruxelles, working with Christophe Petit. I did research in mostly isogeny cryptography, either looking for algorithmic improvements to systems or investigating their security claims. My thesis studied different post-quantum cryptographic group actions.

I did a Masters of Mathematics at the University of Waterloo with David Jao. My thesis was about using SQISign in an adaptor signature.
